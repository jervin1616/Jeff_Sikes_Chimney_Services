What We Offer

Our Services

Full-Service Care

Everything Your Chimney & Fireplace Needs

From routine annual sweeps to complete chimney replacements, Jeff Sikes Chimney Services handles every aspect of chimney and fireplace care for Wilmington homeowners — with honest, upfront pricing on every job.

01
Chimney Cleaning & Sweeping

Professional removal of soot, creosote, debris, and blockages from the firebox, smoke chamber, flue liner, and cap. We keep your system safe and burning efficiently — every season.

02
Chimney Inspection

Thorough Level I & II inspections to identify structural issues, cracking, water infiltration, flue obstructions, and safety hazards. We specialize in real estate and HOA inspections — clear documentation, honest assessments, and results explained before any work begins.

03
Chimney Repair & Firebox Rebuild

Expert masonry repairs including mortar repointing, firebox rebuilds, flashing fixes, spalling brick repair, and exterior crack sealing to restore structural integrity and stop water intrusion.

04
Fireplace Installation

Specializing in factory-built fireplace installation. Whether you're adding a new system or replacing an existing one, we deliver lasting, professional results. We do not offer hearth tile/trim work or cosmetic renovations.

05
Gas Log Service

Expert gas log service, installation, and repair for any gas fireplace brand. We focus on service first — and we can also source and sell gas logs when you need them. No runaround, no middleman.

06
Chimney Caps & Crown Repair

Custom chimney cap fabrication and installation including non-standard sizes. Crown repairs, tuckpointing, and chase cover replacement to protect your chimney from weather and wildlife damage.

07
Waterproofing & Leak Repair

Diagnosing and permanently sealing chimney leaks, water infiltration, and moisture damage. We find the source — whether flashing, crown, mortar, or liner — and fix it right the first time.

08
Dryer Vent Cleaning

Professional dryer vent cleaning to eliminate lint buildup, restore dryer efficiency, and reduce fire risk. A quick, affordable service that makes a significant difference in home safety.

09
Full Chimney Replacement

When repair simply isn't enough, we handle complete chimney replacements from the roofline up. Skilled craftsmanship, proper materials, and upfront pricing so you always know what to expect.
